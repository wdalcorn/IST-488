Alpha Chi Sigma - Pi

About

Since its establishment in 1911, Alpha Chi Sigma, a co-ed professional fraternity, has remained an integral presence on the Syracuse University campus. This organization has upheld a rich tradition of promoting an understanding and enthusiasm for chemistry and its related sciences within both the university campus and the local community.

Additional Information

Description:
The Pi Chapter of Alpha Chi Sigma (ΑΧΣ)—commonly referred to as "AXS"—is the professional chemistry fraternity here at Syracuse University and SUNY ESF. We are the only professional chemistry fraternity, and we are a national organization. Our co-ed group consists of various backgrounds and majors, and we are united by the common interest in the science of chemistry. Alpha Chi Sigma, as a professional organization, engages in service projects that are science-related, as well as non science-related activities. We also like to consider ourselves "part social" because we enjoy planning and participating in social events, like formal and semiformal, movie nights, and dinners.
