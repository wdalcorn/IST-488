Model United Nations Club

About

The Model United Nations Club meets weekly to discuss varied topics of international importance as well as fun fantasy themes from the perspective of diverse countries and parties. We practice a mix of both General Assembly and crisis style committees. Through this club, you will develop the ability to think critically, speak persuasively, and write decisively. It is open to Syracuse and SUNY ESF undergraduate students of any experience level.
