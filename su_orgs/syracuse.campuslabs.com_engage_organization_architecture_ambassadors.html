Description:
The Architecture Ambassadors are a group of undergraduate students that assist in recruiting and admissions efforts. They play an important role in representing the School and University by hosting tours of the facilities to prospective students and their families. Members also assist at campus admission events and career fairs. All current Bachelor of Architecture students are welcome to join. Please contact Dana McQuillan for more information.
